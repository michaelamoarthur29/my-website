Overview

The Problem Ford Gave Us

Ford's research teams were drowning in data. Three researchers spent 40+ hours each to code just 500 survey responses. Research findings needed to reach varying audiences — from engineers to executives — but there was no scalable, consistent way to communicate them.

Our prompt: "Understand the current practices for research documentation and dissemination within Ford and imagine plausible futures (1–3 years out) that leverage generative AI to analyze both quantitative and qualitative data as inputs and enhance reporting with a strong focus on storytelling."

Research findings must be communicated to varying audiences — but the process is slow, inconsistent, and unsustainable at scale.

Process

Three Modules, One Product

01

Module A — Discovery & Strategy

Conducted stakeholder interviews with Ford's research team and secondary research with a 24-hour turnaround. Identified three opportunity areas: AI-accelerated video synthesis, AI for multi-audience communication, and data analysis with human oversight. After evaluation, the team advanced with the layered storytelling approach — speaking to various audiences at once from a single dataset.

02

Module B — Ideation & Prototyping

Brainstormed three product concepts in one week: a video-to-searchable-database transcription tool, a data-to-presentation compiler, and an interactive dashboard tool. Built all three using vibe coding platforms (v0.ai and others), created high-level moodboards for each, and presented to Ford. StoryStack — the interactive dashboard — was selected by Ford as the strongest solution.

03

Module C — Refinement & Brand

Conducted user trials with both Ford and BVCC teams. Assessed risks and costs. Defined the target market and built a user persona around the primary pain point. Created the StoryStack brand identity — including the logo, visual system, and product narrative. Documented all work thoroughly via Notion and Google Drive for handoff.

The Solution

What StoryStack Does

StoryStack is an AI-powered research reporting dashboard. Users upload processed research data, choose a template and audience, set the narrative tone — and StoryStack generates an interactive, audience-aware story from the data. It connects directly to Power BI and Excel for seamless data import.

Executive View

High-level summary tiles with KPIs, supporting quotes, and trend indicators — built for leaders who need insight fast without wading through raw data.

Researcher View

Annotated screens, chart breakdowns, and cross-referenced findings — giving research teams the depth they need with narrative structure built in.

Audience Targeting

Users define their audience before generating a report — StoryStack adapts the tone, depth, and framing of the narrative automatically.

Human + AI Balance

AI handles synthesis and formatting; humans remain in control of validation, context, and final narrative decisions — preserving research integrity.
